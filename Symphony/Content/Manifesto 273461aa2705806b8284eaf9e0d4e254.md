## Our Work Style

### Communication First

- Respond to messages and requests promptly
- If you're busy, acknowledge receipt and provide a timeline
- Use clear, specific language in all communications
- Share updates proactively, especially when facing challenges

### Time as a Shared Resource

- Honor meeting times and deadlines
- Notify the team immediately if circumstances change
- Come prepared to meetings and collaborative sessions
- Respect others' focused work time

### Ownership and Support

- Take full responsibility for your assigned work
- Ask for help when needed, but don't delegate your responsibilities
- Offer assistance to teammates when you can provide value
- Maintain high standards for your contributions

### Continuous Improvement

- Embrace feedback as an opportunity to grow
- Share learnings and insights with the team
- Challenge ideas constructively and respectfully
- Contribute to improving our processes and culture
